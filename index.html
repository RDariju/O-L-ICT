About the classes

I teach O/L Information & Communication Technology for Grade 10 and Grade 11 students in both English and Sinhala mediums. The course follows the O/L ICT syllabus and covers practical computer skills and theoretical knowledge required for the exam.

What you'll learn

  • Computer fundamentals and hardware
  • Word processing, spreadsheets and presentations
  • Basic programming logic and flowcharts
  • Internet, email, and online safety
  • Exam-focused practice and past paper techniques

Who is this for?

This is for students preparing for the O/L ICT subject (Grade 10 & 11). Classes are suitable for beginners and students who want focused revision for exams.
